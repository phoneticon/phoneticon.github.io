Introduction

The aim is of this development plan to build a prototype interface for Phoneticon; a new platform for next-generation digital communication. The prototype interface will feature sufficient functionalities to give the user a taste of creating audiovisual texts, or, phoneticons. The main purpose of Phoneticon is to provide a new platform for text messeging on digital devices, where the expression of the textual content is expanded beyond the choice of font types; to the mediums of audio, and, visual & motion graphics.

By expanding the expressibility of the digital text, the user will be able to create and send messeges that convey more complex meanings by adding emotional contents to the text. The current text messeging platforms have incorporated emojis, GIFs and other visually oriented elements, as well as, audio messeging to expand the user's ability to express all that which cannot be expressed solely by words. Phoneticon approaches the limitations of written language to carry emotional contents by adding audiovisual and motive elements to the text.

By using Phoneticon as a medium for communication, we believe that the users will be able to develop and enhance their creativity, sensibility and empathy. When creating a phoneticon, it requires the user to think and feel about how to visually format, sonically shape and set the motion of the textual content based on the user's conceptual and emotional messege. This requires the user's ability to think creatively, feel for sensibility of aesethetics, and intuit empathetically the emotions that the user wants to convey.
